Questions you should ask

If you don't see yours, call me: (919) 773-6114.

Will it sound robotic?

No. We use the same voice technology (ElevenLabs) as every premium voice-AI product on the market. Most callers can't tell it's AI. Listen to a 30-second sample.

What if it mishandles a call?

Every call's transcript reaches you within 60 seconds. If Joshua handled something wrong, you see it immediately and we tune it — every week of the pilot I personally review every call and adjust the prompts. After the pilot, weekly reviews continue on the Growth tier.

What integrations do you have?

Google Calendar, Calendly, Jobber, ServiceTitan, HubSpot, MailerLite, Zapier. We'll build custom ones on the Vertical Pro tier. Ask if you don't see yours.

What happens to our call data?

Audio is ephemeral — deleted at the end of each session, never stored or used for training. Transcripts are yours, stored on your private Bunn dashboard. We will sign an NDA before any pilot if you prefer. Law firms: we do not retain audio or train on your calls, ever.

What if I want to cancel?

30-day written notice, any time. No contracts. Your phone number stays your phone number — we just stop answering for you. The pilot period has zero commitment.

How quickly can you set it up?

Live on your number within 24 hours of signup. We configure the LiveKit dispatch rule, customize the greeting + vertical prompts for your business, and run a test call with you. Pilot starts the moment it's live.

Are you HIPAA compliant?

Not formally certified yet — if you're a medical practice handling PHI, we'll refer you to a HIPAA-native provider. Law firms: Joshua handles qualification and intake without taking privileged substantive content; sessions are ephemeral. Home services + real estate + accounting: no HIPAA concerns apply.
