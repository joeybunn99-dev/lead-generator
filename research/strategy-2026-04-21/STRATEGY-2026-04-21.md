## Tradeoffs I want you to see before you agree

1. **You're walking away from the healthcare market for 6 months.** BunnTranslator pause means No Barrier / Sully.ai / Viva AI keep building while you don't. If voice-AI-medical-interpretation matters to you long-term, this is the opportunity cost.
2. **Home services is crowded in NC.** RoofingAI, LeadStation, ServiceTitan all serve this segment. You're not the only AI phone pitch this roofer has heard. Differentiation is Clayton-local + personal-touch, not tech.
3. **The "win the race" framing implies beating Retell / your brother's build.** You probably won't beat Retell at feature/price on the platform axis. What you *can* beat them at is customer ownership — NC SMBs trust a Clayton-based vendor they can meet for coffee over a SaaS platform out of SF.
4. **90-day MRR of $2,500 is a ceiling, not a floor.** Solo execution + cold outreach can under-deliver if the email copy misses. Budget one major message pivot between day 20 and day 45 if reply rates are below 2%.
5. **You're betting on managed-service discipline.** The $299/mo pricing only works if you actually review calls weekly. If that slips to monthly, churn goes up. This is a ~3–5 hours/week habit that has to be protected.
